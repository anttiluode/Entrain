ENTRAIN LIVE

A cochlea that grows itself.

Every node is a Stuart–Landau resonator — the same substrate, the same exponential integrator, and the same leaky-integrator surprise gate as core.py / grow.py / clutch.py, integrated sample-accurately inside the audio callback. It starts with one randomly tuned ear. Whistle, hum, or play music: unexplained spectral energy accumulates in the gate, and when it trips, the network grows an ear at the frequency it failed to hear — attached to its nearest kin, harmonics sprouting as a comb. Ears that stop earning their keep are pruned.

Then press HEAR ITS EARS and listen to its percept get richer as it learns. Headphones recommended (it will otherwise hear itself, which is its own kind of experiment).

Honesty note: the FFT is used only to decide where to place the next ear, exactly as in grow.py. Sensing itself is done by the resonators. Nothing is trained; structure is grown.
